Good day. Today, let me tell you about H-CODE, Hyundai Customer-Oriented Dealership Experience.

As you know, nowadays, the quality of automobile brands has become similar, and selling things cheap does not always yield results.

Customers value emotional factors more than rational factors such as quality or price, and prefer ‘experience’ rather than ‘information’.

So it’s important now to provide a differentiated dealership experience to our customers for continued success in the future.
